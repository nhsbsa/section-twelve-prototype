Welcome to the Mental Health Act Approvals Database

This database will give you the approval status for Section 12(2) Approved Doctors, Approved Clinicians and Deprivation of Libery Safeguards (DoLS) Training Doctors in England. It will show their expiry date and contact details that we hold. If any of these contact details are incorrect, please inform your Local Approval Panel Administrator so that amendments can be made. (A list of these are in the menu).

Users will also be able to print reports for their area of Section 12(2), AC, and DOLS Approved Doctors from the reports menu.

The Instruction manual for users is now available in the menu.

The data on this database is updated daily. Please be aware of this if you print lists of approved doctors.

Please treat any information contained in line with your organisations Data Protection Policy. This data should only be used in connection with Section 12(2) / Approved Clinician and DOLs work. It should not be used for Marketing or purposes outside this remit. It should only be distributed to authorised parties. To view details on this database, you should have your own individual login account. Access will be removed if logins are shared.
