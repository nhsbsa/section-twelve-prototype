User instructions

Login

To login:

  • Enter the email address that you have assigned as your user ID.This should all be in lower case without spaces.
  • Enter the password that you have been given.
  • Ensure that the user option is highlighted and press sign in.
  • If you have forgotten your password, please use the“forgotten your password”option to recover your password to your registered email address. (Your approvals administrator will not be able to reset your password over the phone).

Homepage

From the Homepage you are able to:

  • View the contact information for approval offices in England.
  • Search for a practitioner.
  • Run and print a report.
  • Contact the Database Administartor.
  • Change the password (as explained above).
  • See news and updates and view the online user instruction manual.

Doctor search

To search for a particular practitioner use one of the following:

  • Enter their last name in the name box.You can enter all or part of the name. Do not enter first names also, as this will narrow the search too much. We use the names that the practitioner has registered with their regulatory body.E.gGMC,NMC.
    Then press Search.
  • Enter their professional registration number,i.etheirGMC number and press Search.
  • Enter the Town and a range (you can choose either 1,2,3,4,5,10,25,or 50 Mile radius). This will give you a list of practitioners that meet those parameters.
